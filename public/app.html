We are uncovering the counterpart to Agile as it applies to developing products for the market.
Through this work we have come to value:

Clarity of ownership over gaining consensus
Org-Product congruency over static teams
Long-term thinking over speed to market
Strategic differentiation over competition neutralization

That is, while there is value in the items on the right, we value the items on the left more.
